What We Do

Full-Spectrum Author Promotion

Every service is designed to build visibility, credibility, and commercial momentum for your book — across every platform that matters.

📖

Book Marketing Strategy

End-to-end campaign architecture tailored to your genre, audience, and market. We design the full promotional arc — from pre-launch buzz to long-tail discoverability — ensuring your book reaches the readers who will love it most.

🔍

Amazon Metadata Optimization

Surgical keyword research, category selection, and backend metadata tuning that positions your book exactly where Amazon's algorithm rewards visibility. We reverse-engineer bestseller rankings so your book competes intelligently.

🎬

Cinematic Book Trailers

Hollywood-grade book trailers scored with symphonic orchestral music, composed with narrative pacing and emotional architecture. These aren't slideshows — they're 90-second cinematic experiences that make readers feel your story before they turn a single page.

🎨

Kindle Thumbnail Design

Scroll-stopping thumbnail designs optimized for the 120×187 pixel battlefield of Amazon search. We design for the algorithm and the human eye simultaneously, maximizing click-through rate at every impression.

📊

Amazon A+ Content & Optimization

Enhanced brand content, optimized product descriptions, and strategic pricing architecture that transforms your Amazon listing from a page into a sales conversion machine. We handle A+ modules, editorial reviews, and listing psychology.

✍️

Synopsis Revamping

Your synopsis is the single most important piece of sales copy your book will ever have. We rewrite it with emotional hooks, genre-appropriate pacing, and psychological triggers that convert browsers into buyers.

📱

TikTok BookTok Promotion

Strategic placement within the BookTok ecosystem — the single most powerful organic discovery engine for books today. We create viral-ready content, partner with micro-influencers, and engineer the aesthetic moments that BookTok amplifies.

🏆

Bestseller Strategies

Category-specific launch coordination designed to hit Amazon bestseller tags within your first critical window. We orchestrate review velocity, promotional stacking, price pulsing, and cross-platform traffic funneling to engineer chart placement.

⚙️

Metadata & SEO Optimization

Beyond Amazon — we optimize your book's discoverability across Google, Goodreads, library catalogues, and international retailer platforms. BISAC codes, thema subjects, ISBN metadata, and long-tail keyword architecture across all retail surfaces.

⭐

Review & Social Proof Service

Strategic reader community placement that generates authentic reviews, Goodreads ratings, and editorial endorsements. We build the social proof ecosystem that makes new readers trust your book before they even read the first page.

📣

Social Media & Influencer Marketing

Cross-platform campaigns spanning Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, YouTube, and literary blogs. We match your book to the right influencers, design visual content calendars, and create campaigns that generate genuine reader engagement.

✉️

Email Marketing & Newsletters

Automated email sequences, newsletter features, and reader list development that create a direct, owned channel between you and your readers — independent of any algorithm's whims. Drip campaigns, launch blasts, and ARC distribution.

🌐

Author Website & Branding

Complete author brand identity — from website design to visual language, press kits, and media-ready assets. We create the professional ecosystem that makes publishers, media, and readers take you seriously at first glance.

📚

Goodreads Campaign Management

Full-service Goodreads strategy including profile optimization, librarian-verified listings, targeted giveaway campaigns, reading list placements, and community engagement that drives organic ratings and genre visibility.

🖼️

Cinematic Book Mockups & Posters

Breathtaking, movie-poster-quality visual mockups that position your book as a cinematic event. AI-enhanced environments, thematic staging, and premium compositing that transforms your cover into a work of art worth sharing.

🎞️

Author Legacy Reel

A cinematic mini-documentary capturing your story as an author — your journey, your motivations, your artistic vision. Designed for press kits, festival submissions, radio interviews, and the permanent archive of your creative legacy.

🌍

International Market Expansion

Multilingual campaign deployment across French, German, English, and Spanish-speaking markets. We localize your messaging, optimize for regional Amazon stores, and connect your book to readers who will never find you through English-only channels.

📰

Press Kit Development

Media-ready professional press kits containing your biography, high-resolution author photos, book synopses, review excerpts, cinematic assets, and interview talking points — everything a journalist, podcaster, or festival coordinator needs to feature you.
